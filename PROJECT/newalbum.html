Hit Me Hard and Soft

Hit Me Hard and Soft is the third studio album by American singer-songwriter Billie Eilish, released on May 17, 2024, through Darkroom and Interscope Records. It is her first full-length studio album release since 2021's Happier Than Ever. Eilish co-wrote the album with her brother and frequent collaborator Finneas, who also produced the album.

Hit Me Hard and Soft received critical acclaim, with critics praising it for its songwriting, experimental production, and concise runtime. It topped album charts in 12 countries, including Australia, Germany, New Zealand, and the United Kingdom. To support the album, Eilish will embark on the Hit Me Hard and Soft: The Tour in September 2024.
